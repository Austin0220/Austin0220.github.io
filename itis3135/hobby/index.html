What Are Nixie Tubes?

Nixie Tubes are glass tubes sealed with low pressure neon and argon gas that use high voltage electrical discharge, being the orange glow they are known for, to display information. Usually this information pertains to numbers, but depending on the tube, can display decimal points, mathematical symbols, or even letters.

Why Clocks?

The reason people make clocks out of nixie tubes is mainly due to the visual asthetic of a nixie tube relative to other types of display technology. Sometimes you just can't beat that analogue glow!
