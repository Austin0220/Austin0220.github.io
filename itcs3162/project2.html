Project 2

Premise

The Premise of training this data is twofold. The first component is testing a classification Criteria In the Data itself, being the "Class" parameter. The second is the actual testing and Categorization of rows based on factors relating to physical fitness, i.e. % body fat, jump height, weight, etc.

Methodology

Overall method will be a decision tree, as the data lends itself well to categorization, in addition to being fantastic for testing the "Class" Field.
